About Sud0

I'm Walter O. Owuor, a passionate ethical hacker and automation whiz from Kenya. Currently sharpening my skills on HTB, THM and NETCAD, graduated United States International University (USIU-Africa) in Ethical Hacking Essentials. My focus is on building secure systems and exposing vulnerabilities using Python, Bash, and open-source tools before malicious actors can exploit them.

I run a community cyber initiative, Wlan0tto Cyber Hub, offering device setups, essential digital safety training, and scam busting services. I believe in accessible cybersecurity knowledge. Let's collaborate to strengthen the digital infrastructure, one system at a time.

My Mission

To bridge the gap between technology and security for individuals and small businesses in Kenya.
